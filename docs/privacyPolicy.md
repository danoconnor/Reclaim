# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** March 23rd, 2026

## Overview
Reclaim (“the App”) is designed to help users manage their photo storage by identifying photos that have already been synced to cloud storage. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Information We Collect
We do **not collect, store, or transmit personal data** to any external servers.

All data accessed by the App is processed **locally on your device only**.

## Account Authentication
The App uses Microsoft OneDrive authentication provided by Microsoft.

- You sign in through Microsoft’s secure login system  
- The App does **not** see or store your password  
- Authentication is handled entirely by Microsoft  

For more information on how Microsoft handles your data, please review their Privacy Policy:  
https://privacy.microsoft.com/privacystatement

## Data Accessed by the App

To function, the App requests access to:

### 1. OneDrive Data
Via Microsoft Graph APIs, the App may access:
- File names and metadata  
- Photo and video files stored in your OneDrive  

This data is used only to determine whether local photos have been backed up.

### 2. Device Photos
Using Apple’s system frameworks, the App accesses:
- Photos and videos stored on your device  
- Metadata such as timestamps and file identifiers  

This access is used solely to compare local files with cloud-stored files.

## How Your Data Is Used
All data is used exclusively for:

- Comparing local photos with cloud backups  
- Helping you identify files that can be safely removed from your device  

All processing happens **on-device** using Apple-provided APIs.

## Data Storage and Transmission

- The App does **not store your data on external servers**  
- The App does **not transmit your personal data to the developer**  
- Any communication with OneDrive occurs directly between your device and Microsoft’s services  

## Third-Party Services

The App relies on:

- Microsoft (for authentication and OneDrive access via Microsoft Graph)

These services operate under their own privacy policies.

## Data Sharing

We do **not**:
- Sell your data  
- Share your data with third parties  
- Use your data for advertising or tracking  

## Your Control

You have full control over your data:

- You may revoke OneDrive access at any time through your Microsoft account  
- You may revoke photo access in your device settings  
- You may delete the App at any time  

## Children’s Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect or store personal data from anyone, including children under 13.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by the “Last Updated” date above.
